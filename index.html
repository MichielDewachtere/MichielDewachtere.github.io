I'm Michiel Dewachtere, I'm a game development student from Belgium. I currently study at Digital Arts and Entertainment in Kortrijk, Belgium. I've always been fascinated by how people could create entire worlds from just ones and zeros, and now I can happily say that I can do the same.

My Projects

  • RealEngine

    A small game engine I've made over a solid 12 weeks. It heavily uses some of the patterns discussed in the book Game Programming Patterns by Robert Nystrom. The engine is designed for 2D arcade games, but I would love to revisit it to make it even bigger and more accessible.

    With the engine, there also comes a replica of the arcade game "Burger Time" showcasing the engine's abilities.

  • Corporate Chaos

    Corporate Chaos is a 2-4 player couch pvp racing game developed during my time at DAE. The team consisted out of 2 programmers and 3 artists. My job mainly focussed on all the pick-ups and the environmental obstacles. The HUD, camera and menu's were also implemented by me. The game was designed and made in 12 weeks.
